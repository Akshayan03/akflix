# Legal Notice

Akflix is a media **client**. It contains no media, no torrent trackers and
no indexers. It connects to services that **you** run and configure:

- **Jellyfin** — plays media from your own server/library.
- **qBittorrent** — a general-purpose BitTorrent client.
- **Prowlarr** — an indexer manager; it ships empty and only searches
  indexers you explicitly add.

## Your responsibilities

1. **BitTorrent is a legal, neutral protocol.** What you download with it may

   rightsholder's permission is illegal in most jurisdictions and can carry
   civil and criminal penalties.
2. **You are solely responsible** for the indexers you configure, the content
   you search for, download, stream, seed, or import into your library, and
   for complying with the laws of your country and the terms of your ISP.
3. **Seeding redistributes content.** Remember that BitTorrent uploads while
   it downloads — sharing unauthorized copyrighted content is itself
   infringement, independent of downloading.

## Legitimate uses

There is plenty of content you can torrent lawfully, e.g.:

- Public-domain films and archives (Internet Archive, Prelinger, etc.)
- Creative Commons–licensed media (e.g. Blender Foundation open movies such
  as *Big Buck Bunny*, *Sintel*, *Tears of Steel*)
- Linux distributions and other open-source software
- Content you created, or that you are explicitly licensed to redistribute
