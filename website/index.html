The feature presentation

Jellyfin underneath.
Opening night on top.

Every capability is wired to your own server and your own torrent stack — Akflix ships with no accounts, no tracking, and no content.

Netflix-grade interface

Hero banners, buttery rows, hover previews, loading skeletons and a dark house style that gets out of the film's way.

Jellyfin native

Your libraries, users, subtitles, transcoding and watch progress — kept in sync with every other Jellyfin client you own.

Torrent search built in

Query every Prowlarr indexer you've configured from inside the app and hand results straight to qBittorrent.

Stream while it downloads

Sequential piece order makes files watchable in minutes; one click scans them into your Jellyfin library.

The show follows you

Leave the player and playback drops into a floating picture-in-picture card with a now-playing bar. Click to go full screen again.

Profiles & projection booth

Multiple servers and users behind a “Who's watching?” switcher, a system tray to hide into, and signed auto-updates.
