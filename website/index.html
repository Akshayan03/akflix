Good to know

Straight answers.

No mystery dependencies or fake promises about how peer networks behave.

Do I need Jellyfin, Docker, or qBittorrent?

No. The standalone Mac build includes its playback engine and FFmpeg. Jellyfin and external qBittorrent connections are optional advanced settings.

Does Watch Now download the whole movie first?

No. Akflix prioritizes the selected video and streams it through a seekable local URL. Temporary pieces are cached during playback and removed afterward. Download is a separate option.

Can playback always be Netflix-instant?

A hosted or debrid-cached HTTP link can start almost immediately. A public peer source cannot be guaranteed instant because its speed depends on metadata, reachable peers, seeders, and your connection. Akflix removes avoidable waiting, but it cannot manufacture a healthy peer.

Does it support shows?

Yes. The standalone catalog includes both movies and series, with season and episode selection before Watch Now.

What about Intel, Windows, and Linux?

The new zero-setup package is currently available for Apple-silicon Macs. Earlier cross-platform builds remain on GitHub Releases while standalone packaging is completed for those systems.
